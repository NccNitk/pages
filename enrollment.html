Application

A student desirous of being enrolled as cadet in 2 KAR ENGR COY NCC should apply through online.The application is open till 1700hrs 22nd Dec 2020

APPLY

Eligibility

  1. Applicant should be citizen of India or a subject of Nepal.
  2. Should bear good moral character.
  3. Should be a student in National Institute of Technology Karnataka(or any institute under 2 KAR ENGR COY NCC).
  4. Should meet the medical standards.
  5. Age below 26 years.
  6. Should be a student in NITK for next 3 years.

Vacancies Available.

2 KAR ENGR COY NCC have an authorized strength of 105 SDs and 55 SWs. The vacancy available for enrollment depends on the number of cadets in second and third year NCC.

Selection

A applicant can only be enrolled to 2 KAR ENGR COY NCC only after clearing the Selection procedures which will include personal interview, physical tests, etc. The selection procedure will be decided OC and ANOs and may change every year.
